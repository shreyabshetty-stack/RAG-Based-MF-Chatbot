What is the exit load of HDFC Equity Fund?
auto_awesome
check_circle Verified Answer

For the HDFC Equity Fund Direct Growth, the exit load structure is as follows:

  • An exit load of 1.00% is applicable if units are redeemed or switched out within 1 year from the date of allotment.
  • Nil exit load applies if units are redeemed or switched out after 1 year from the date of allotment.

Note: Always verify against the latest Scheme Information Document (SID) as fund houses may revise these charges.
